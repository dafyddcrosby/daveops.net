===== Setting ciphers on an SSL listener =====
--------------------
::

  bind :443 ssl no-sslv3 crt /path/to/cert.pem ciphers ALL:!EXPORT:!aNULL:!eNULL:!RC4:!MD5:!DES:!3DES:!MEDIUM:!WEAK
