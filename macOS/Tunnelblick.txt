====== Tunnelblick ======
===========
:date: 2015-03-05

===== CLI =====
---
::

 # Connect to a single VPN
 osascript -e 'Tell app "Tunnelblick" to connect "example-vpn"'
 # Disconnect all VPNs
 osascript -e 'Tell app "Tunnelblick" to disconnect all'
